The Artist

A passion for finding beauty in everyday objects.

Christophe Poly finds inspiration in volume, structure and form — fed by travel, fashion, design and architecture. Over the years he's developed a style and a technique that are entirely his own.

Each season, collections are refined and new designs surprise a loyal clientele. Today his jewelry can be found in galleries, boutiques and museums across North America.

The Studio

An architectural approach, from raw material to finished piece.

i

The Material

Aluminum, brass, acrylic — chosen for their volume, weight and how they hold up over time.

ii

The Cut

Each plate is cut to proportions considered the way one would consider a structure.

iii

The Assembly

Elements are filed, adjusted and combined by hand until the balance feels right.

iv

The Finish

Every piece is inspected individually before it leaves the studio.
